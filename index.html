Teleport means "to replace yourself wherever you want in one second".

What is happening at protest march in Cairo right now? Ten live minutes of your friend's wedding 10 000 km away? How many cars are at a traffic jam on the way to office?

Watch live broadcasting from everywhere. See various events all over the world with your own eyes.

Instructions how to use teleport.

For a viewer: look at the translation sceldue in advance or just choose any place on the Earth to find live videos. Communicate with a cameraman to say what you want to see.

For a uploader: put upcoming translation at the sceldue. At the appointed time broadcast things around you to the Internet!
